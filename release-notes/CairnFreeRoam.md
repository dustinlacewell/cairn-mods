## CairnFreeRoam v0.1.0

- Unlocks the game's eagle-eye fast-travel in every save — campaign, free-solo, anything, not just Free Roam.
- Reveals every warp point on the map, including bivouacs you haven't rested at yet.
- Add your own bookmarks: in the eagle-eye view, bookmark wherever you're standing (B), then rename (R) or delete (Del) them. Bookmarks persist across saves.
- Pure UI/selection unlock — no save data is touched, and everything is reversible from the config.
