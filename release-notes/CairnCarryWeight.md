## CairnCarryWeight v1.0.0

- Heavier pack tires you faster: per-limb climbing effort scales with how full your bag is — 1x empty up to a tunable max (default 2x) at full.
- Per-item weight overrides: make specific items heavier or lighter (affects bag capacity, the weight readout, and stamina drain).
- Configurable in-game on the Mods settings page: master toggle, full-bag multiplier, item overrides, and a live reload.
