## CairnCarryWeight v0.1.0

- Heavier pack tires you faster: the fuller your bag, the more stamina climbing costs — vanilla when empty, up to 5x at full (default 2x, tunable).
- Live carry-weight readout on the bag screen.
- Per-item weight overrides — a true override affecting bag capacity, the weight readout, and the stamina cost together.
- Per-climber weight, so it's co-op safe.
- Configurable in-game on the Mods page: master toggle, full-bag multiplier, item overrides, and a live reload.
