# Cairn

## What is this game?

Cairn is a climbing game by The Game Bakers. You are Aava, an alpinist
attempting the first ascent of Mount Kami — free-climbing, placing pitons,
managing food, water, injuries and rest on the way to the summit.

## What does randomization do to this game?

Story beats along the route become location checks: as you climb past each
milestone of the ascent, you send checks to the multiworld. Inventory items
(food, drink, medicine, climbing gear) are shuffled into the multiworld item
pool and arrive from other players. Progressive Altitude Permits gate how far
up the mountain you are allowed to climb.

## What is the goal?

Reach the summit of Mount Kami.
